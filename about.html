About Elegant Cuisine

Founded in 2010 by Chef Isabella Martinez, Elegant Cuisine began with a simple yet profound vision: to transform dining into an art form that engages all the senses. What started as a small, intimate establishment with just twelve tables has evolved into one of the most celebrated culinary destinations in New York City.

Our journey has been guided by an unwavering commitment to culinary excellence, innovative techniques, and an atmosphere that balances luxury with warmth. Each dish tells a story‚Äîof tradition, of creativity, of the finest ingredients sourced from around the world and from our local partners.

Meet Our Founder

Chef Isabella Martinez

Executive Chef & Founder

Chef Isabella's culinary journey began in her grandmother's kitchen in Barcelona, where she developed a profound appreciation for the way food brings people together. After formal training at the Culinary Institute of America and apprenticeships under Michelin-starred chefs in Paris and Tokyo, she honed a distinctive style that marries classical techniques with bold innovation.

Her approach to cuisine is deeply influenced by her travels across five continents, where she studied indigenous ingredients and traditional methods. "Food is a universal language," she often says. "In my kitchen, we speak it with respect, passion, and a touch of daring."

When not in the restaurant, Chef Isabella dedicates her time to the Elegant Foundation, which provides culinary education to underprivileged youth and advocates for sustainable food systems.

Our Philosophy

At Elegant Cuisine, we believe that dining is more than just eating‚Äîit's an experience that engages all the senses. Our culinary approach centers around four core principles:

  • Exceptional Ingredients: We source only the finest seasonal ingredients, building relationships with local farmers, fishermen, and artisanal producers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.
  • Artful Preparation: Our techniques honor traditional methods while embracing innovation, resulting in dishes that surprise and delight with every bite.
  • Meticulous Presentation: Each plate is a canvas, thoughtfully composed to create visual harmony that enhances the dining experience.
  • Impeccable Service: Our staff is trained to provide attentive yet unobtrusive service, ensuring your experience is seamless from the moment you arrive until your departure.

Our Space

Designed by acclaimed architect Marco Visconti, our dining space reflects our culinary philosophy through its blend of classic elegance and contemporary sensibility. The open kitchen serves as the centerpiece of our restaurant, allowing guests to witness the culinary artistry in action, while intimate seating arrangements create an atmosphere of both elegance and comfort.

Our dining room features handcrafted wooden tables, custom lighting that casts a warm glow, and floor-to-ceiling windows that overlook our meticulously maintained herb garden. The space accommodates 60 guests, ensuring personalized attention for each diner.
